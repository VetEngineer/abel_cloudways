== Description ==

Festiva is the most versatile events and conference theme for WordPress. It has 04 unique homepage layouts, available as multi-page or one-page designs. Each layout has been carefully crafted to provide a modern and clean browsing experience for your readers. With Festiva, your events and conferences are going to stand out like never before.
